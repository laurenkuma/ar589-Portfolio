50 ITERATIONS

This project was focused on re-creating 50 different posters inspired by one chosen poster. Therefore, I chose The Language of Michael Graves by William Longhauser. The Language of Michael Graves was based on his postmodern architect drawings. Coming from the architectures, I tried to maintain structured feelings in my booklet. I used vellum one of the material forthis book pages since numerous architects use vellum for their architecture drawings.
